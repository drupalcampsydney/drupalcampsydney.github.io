Drupal Camp Sydney 2014

Blue Mountains YHA, Katoomba

28th - 29th June 2014

Drupal Camp Sydney 2014 is being held over two days in the Blue Mountains in June. Make the trip up the mountains to share a weekend with fellow Drupal enthusiasts sharing their knowledge and ideas.

The venue

The venue is the YHA Katoomba which is conveniently located just down from the main drag and easy walking distance to the Three Sisters, the station, restaurants and other local attractions.

The location of the camp is a large single room which can easily accommodate 40 Drupalistas. There are "cozy corner" couches to chill out on, a hackers area for collaborative working, a presentation area with projector and a kitchen which we have to ourselves. We have access to the room 24/7 so it is available for late night hack fests if you are up for that kind of thing.

Cost

We are keeping the cost of the event to a minimum for participants. Tickets cost $20 per day. A ticket includes:

  • entry to the event,
  • drinks and snacks throughout the day,
  • wifi,
  • lunch and
  • a drink at the bar on Saturday night

Accommodation

You will have to sort out your own accommodation. We highly recommend the venue itself, the YHA. There are dorm rooms and a limited number of double rooms available - all at very reasonable prices. A dorm room is available from around $36. There are some family rooms available as well. Get in quick if you would like to book one of these. Please book directly with the venue (+61 (0)24782 1416).

Unconference

The camp will be relatively unstructured. If you would like to present, the presentation area is available for short talks less than 30mins long. Lightning talks welcome

The room will have tables divided into little pods for like minded hackers:

  • frontend,
  • developers,
  • help and
  • ad hoc bofs.

Whatever your Drupal interest, you're sure to find some collaborators.

Who is this for?

Will you want to attend this event? We hope so.

It is open to old hands and new comers alike. Drupal people love helping others and sharing knowledge. It's a good as place as any to get your Drupal fix. If you are evaluating Drupal and want to check out the community it is also for you. We'll be happy to talk through what you want to get out of Drupal... and what you can give back too :)

You make the camp

If you have any cool ideas for the camp then please be sure to contact us. You might like to:

  • make a presentation,
  • give a training session,
  • lead a sprint on a project,
  • organise a BOF,
  • provide help to learners,
  • help with organistion and promotion,
  • sponsor the event.

Not for profit

The event is being run in a not for profit way. We don't expect any left over money at its conclusion. If there is anything left, the funds will be returned to the Sydney Meetup community in some way.
