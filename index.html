Drupal Camp Sydney 2014

Drupal Camp Sydney 2014 is being held over two days in the Blue Mountains in June. Make the trip up the mountains to share a weekend with fellow Drupal enthusiasts sharing their knowledge and ideas.

The venue

The venue is YHA at Katoomba which is conveniently located just down from the main drag and easy walking distance to the Three Sisters and other local attractions.

The location of the camp is a large single room which can easily accommodate 40 Drupalistas. There are "cozy corner" couches to chill out on, a hackers area for collaborative working, a presentation area with projector and a kitchen which we have to ourselves. We have access to the room 24/7 so it is available for late night hack fests if you are up for that kind of thing.

Tickets

The event is limited in size to 40 people. Please book early to avoid disappointment. Ticket sales will be opened soon - an announcement will be made once available.

Cost

We are keeping the cost of the event to a minimum for participants. Tickets cost $20 per day. A ticket includes:

  • entry to the event,
  • drinks and snacks throughout the day,
  • wifi,
  • lunch and
  • a drink at the bar on Saturday night

You will have to sort out your own accommodation though. We highly recommend staying at the YHA itself. There are dorm rooms and a limited number of double rooms available - all at very reasonable prices. A dorm room is available from around $36. Please book your accommodation early to avoid disappointment.

Unconference

The camp will be relatively unstructured. The presentation are is available for short talks less than 30mins long. There will also be room for lightning talks at the end of Saturday. If you would like to present, please register your interest.

The room will have tables divided into little pods for like minded hackers:

  • frontend,
  • developers,
  • help and
  • ad hoc bofs.

Whatever your Drupal interest, you're sure to find some collaborators.

Who is this for?

Will you want to attend this event? We hope so.

It is open to old hands and new comers alike. Drupal people love helping others and sharing knowledge. It's a good as place as any to get your Drupal fix. If you are evaluating Drupal and want to check out the community it is also for you. We'll be happy to talk through what you want to get out of Drupal... and what you can give back too :)

You make the camp

If you have any cool ideas for the camp then please be sure to get in touch with us. This could include:

  • A presentation you would like to make
  • A training session on a particular area
  • A project the group can collaborate on
  • A bof you wish to organise
  • Help you would like to give.

Sponsors

We are currently looking for four sponsors to cover the fixed costs of the event including room hire for two days, snacks and bar. If you would like to sponsor please in touch. Sponsorship packages are $250. You'll get a logo at the bottom of this page and a mention on the day.

Not for profit

The event is being run in a not for profit way. We don't expect any left over money at its conclusion. If there is anything left, the funds will be returned to the Sydney Meetup community in some way.
